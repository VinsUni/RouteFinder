Route description

Route 146 starts in Pleasant Grove at the intersection of 100 East and State 
StreetUS-89 . Travelling north on 100 
East, the route leaves Pleasant Grove, turns into Canyon Road, and travels 
along the eastern foothills ofAmerican Fork 
 until ending at the Alpine 
Loop Scenic Byway  at 
the mouth of American Fork Canyon.
