Route description

SR-88 begins at the south end of the Green River 
 bridge near Ouray 
, 
within theUintah Basin . It heads 
northwards, gradually climbing through the valley of that river, passing east of
Pelican Lake 

, and onto a more ruggedplateau  just 
beyond the intersection with 4500 South (which carried SR-88 until 1969). The 
route's north end is atUS-40 /US-191 
 on this plateau.
