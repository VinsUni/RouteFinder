Route description

The route begins at the intersection of 400 North (SR-147 
) and Childs Road in Spanish Fork 
, SR-51 heading northeast as a 
two-lane undivided highway on the latter. As the road exits Spanish Fork, it 
heads into a rural portion ofUtah County 
. The continues northeast until 
1600 North south ofSpringville 
, where the road turns north. The road continues north until terminating at a 
grade-separated intersection withUS-89 
, one of the oldest grade-separated intersections inUtah 
.
