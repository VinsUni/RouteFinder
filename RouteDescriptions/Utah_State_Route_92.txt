Route description

   
 A view of
Mount Timpanogos  from the 
Alpine Loop The route runs east from Highland and up American Fork Canyon 
, where it enters the Uinta 
National Forest . Shortly 
after, it passesTimpanogos Cave National Monument 
, a famous 
group of caves buried in the side of the canyon. The route continues up the 
canyon and bends to the south where it passes the east side ofMount Timpanogos 
. After passing Sundance Ski 
Resort, the road links up with US-189 inProvo Canyon 
.
