Route description

The route begins at the intersection of Clark Lane in a semi-developed 
portion of Farmington and heads north on Park Lane as a four-lane road. About 
400 feet (120Â m) north of the junction, the route turns northeast to intersect 
at I-15, where there is an off-ramp from southbound I-15 on exit 325 and an 
on-ramp from SR-225 onto northbound I-15. Immediately after, SR-225 comes to 
another interchange, this time with the US-89 freeway, which includes access to 
southbound I-15. Past this intersection, the route heads due west before 
intersecting an access road toLagoon Amusement Park 
 and terminating at SR-106.[
