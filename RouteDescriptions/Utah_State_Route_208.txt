Route description

The route begins on US-40  about 6 
miles (9.7 km) east ofFruitland  
in theUintah Basin  region of the 
state. 
 
From there, it travels north approximately 8 miles (13 km) before turning to 
the east and winding down Golden Stair Canyon. After exiting the canyon, the 
route turns back to the north as it crosses theDuchesne River 
 and ends at its intersection with 
SR-35  just southeast of Tabiona 
.
