Route description

From its western terminus at exit 84 of I-80 
, northeast of the Stansbury Mountains 
, outside any city limits, 
the route heads southwest (toward the mountain range), then turns 
southeasterly. Upon entering the western side ofGrantsville 
, the highway heads east 
(serving as Grantsville's Main Street) and turns to the northeast after leaving 
the city. It runs northeasterly until terminating atMills Junction 
 north of Stansbury Park 
, at milepost 62.9 of 
Highway 36.
