Route description

The route begins at a partial diamond interchange 
 at I-15 
 on exit 317. The highway heads east on 
400 North, a four-lane undivided highway. The route continues throughBountiful 
 and turns north on Main Street, 
a two-lane undivided road. The route continues north intoCenterville 
. North of Chase Lane, the road 
begins turning northwest. By Lund Lane, the road heads due north again. By this 
point, the route becomes 200 East. As the highway entersFarmington 
, 200 East becomes State Street. 
Immediately after, the route turns north on Main Street. The route then turns 
northwest and continues in this manner until a diamond interchange withUS-89 
 at exit 396, where it terminates.
