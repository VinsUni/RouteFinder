Route description

The entire length of SR-60 is just south of the Weber River 
 and north of the Davis-Weber Canal 

 in the cities ofRiverdale  and 
South Weber . The highway begins 
atSR-26  (Riverdale Road) and heads 
south underI-84 , turning southeast at 
theSR-168  intersection and slowly 
climbing towards the end atUS-89 . 
Cornia Drive - formerlySR-49A 
 - 
continues straight at the east terminus, an interchange withUS-89 
 near the mouth of the Weber Canyon 
. Traffic wanting to continue east 
through the canyon, leaving theWeber Valley 
 
to cross theWasatch Range , heads 
north on US-89 to I-84.
