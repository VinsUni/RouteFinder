Route description

SR-90 begins in the center of Brigham City and heads east through the 
east-central portion of the town. After continuing east for six blocks, the 
highway veers to the southeast to meet with US-89/US-91 at the mouth of Brigham 
Canyon, where SR-90 traffic is diverted to US-89/US-91 northbound through a 
flyover ramp.
