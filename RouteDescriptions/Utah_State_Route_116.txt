Route description

SR-116 begins at an intersection with SR-132 in eastern Moroni. It serves as 
a continuation of Main Street as it exits Moroni. The route then turns 
northeast with farmland to the south and desert to the north. Turning back 
east, SR-116 crosses the San Pitch River, fields begin to appear north of the 
route. The highway then enters Mount Pleasant, taking on the name of Main 
Street and ending at US-89 in the center of the city.
