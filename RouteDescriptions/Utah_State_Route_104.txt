Route description

From the western terminus at SR-126 
 (1900 West) inOgden , the route is 
named 21st Street, a standard two-way, east–westminor arterial 
, with an interchange with I-15 
 near the west end. This section of the 
route is about 2 miles (3.2 km) long. About half a mile west of the eastern 
terminus atSR-204  (Wall Ave), as it 
starts to pass over railroad tracks, the route splits into aone-way pair 
, with the eastbound lanes 
continuing as 21st street, and the westbound lanes located one block to the 
north as 20th Street.
