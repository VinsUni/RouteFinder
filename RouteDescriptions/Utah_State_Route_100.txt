Route description

The route begins as 400 North at SR-99 
 (Main Street) in Fillmore 
. From here, it travels west out 
of town, crosses overI-15 . After 4.6 
miles (7.4 km), the route turns north and continues through rural Millard 
County for about 12.3 miles (19.8 km) until it ends atUS-50 
.
