Route description

The route's southern end is in the town of Paradise in the southeast corner 
of theCache Valley . 
 
Starting in the center of the town at the intersection of Bridger Boulevard and 
9100 South, the route travels west for two blocks before turning north along 
200 West. The route leaves Paradise, continuing in a northerly direction along 
the eastern part of the valley, passing through the city of Hyrum as 800 West, 
through the city of Nibley as Nibley Road, before merging withUS-89 
/US-91 
 as Main Street just south of the 
center of Logan, thecounty seat  and 
largest city in Cache County.
