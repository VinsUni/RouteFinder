Route description

The road begins at U.S. Route 191 
 in Bluff 
 in a desert area. Paralleling the 
San Juan River  
along Mission Road, the road winds eastward along the north border of theNavajo 
Indian Reservation . 
Travelling through the city ofMontezuma Creek 
, the road intersects State 
Route 262 . The route then 
travels through the city ofAneth  
along the Trail of the Ancients, entering theNavajo Indian Reservation 
. SH 162 meets its east 
end at theColorado  state line, where 
State Highway 41  
continues into theUte Mountain Indian Reservation 
.
