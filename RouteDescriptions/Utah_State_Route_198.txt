Route description

From its western terminus at Interstate 15 
 in Santaquin, the route 
heads north-northeast until reaching Payson, where it straightens out to the 
north. This portion of the route is 100 West in Payson. Soon after, the highway 
turns east on State Road. The highway veers northeast and eventually to the 
north-northeast until Spanish Fork, where it runs north again. The route turns 
east on Canyon Road, eventually turning to the southeast until reaching US-6, 
where it terminates.
