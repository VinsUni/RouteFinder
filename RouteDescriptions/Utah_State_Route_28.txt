Route description

SR-28 begins at an intersection with US-89 
 in central Gunnison 
 and heads north and northwest to 
Fayette . It continues northwest 
past the Sevier Bridge Reservoir and northeast past the Skinner Peaks and Horse 
Heaven Mountain toLevan . 


SR-28 then continues north through Levan to Nephi, where it intersects I-15 
 at the south end of town 
and continues north along the I-15 Business Loop through Nephi, ending at an 
intersection with I-15 north of town.
