Route description

From its western terminus in Francis, the highway runs southeast to the 
junction of Soapstone Basin Road, where it turns south. Afterwards it heads in 
an easterly direction toHanna , where 
it turns southeast and then east to the eastern terminus nearDuchesne 
. Located less than an hour from 
Downtown Salt Lake City, this route is a favorite day ride for many 
Wasatch-front locals.
