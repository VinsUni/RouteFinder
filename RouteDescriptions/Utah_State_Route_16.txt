Route description

SR-16 begins at the Wyoming state line south of Woodruff, as an extension of 
WYO 89 , and heads northwest 
ten miles (16 km) to Woodruff, where it turns north through the town center. 


SR-16 then continues north ten miles (16 km) to Randolph 
, running through the town center. 
North of Randolph, it runs generally north-northeast nine miles (14 km) to end 
at an intersection withSR-30  
at Sage Creek Junction.
