Route description

SR-119 begins on the eastern outskirts of Richfield 
 at SR-118 
 (former US-89 
), which follows 300 North east from 
downtown Richfield and curves northeast towardsSigurd 
. The western half of SR-119 is a 
straight flat road through theSevier Valley 
. After it reaches Main Street, 
which heads south into the town ofGlenwood 
, the highway begins to curve and 
climb, crossing theRainbow Hills 
 
before ending atSR-24  near the mouth 
ofKings Meadow Canyon 

.
