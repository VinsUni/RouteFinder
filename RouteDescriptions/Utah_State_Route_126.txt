Route description

After its southern terminus, the route heads northwest in Layton 
. After entering Clearfield 
, the route turns straight to 
the north. It continues this direction throughSunset 
, Roy 
, and West Haven 
 before veering to the northwest 
when the highway reachesMarriott-Slaterville 
. The route again 
reverts to a northerly direction before reachingFarr West 
. Just before terminating in 
South Willard , the route 
turns northwest one final time.

The entirety of the route, with the exception of a small portion from the 
Interstate 15 /Interstate 84
  junction to the northern 
terminus, lies west of the aforementioned interstates.
