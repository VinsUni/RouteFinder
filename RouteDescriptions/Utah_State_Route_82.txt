Route description

State Route 82 starts at SR-102  
(Main Street) inTremonton  and 
travels north as 300 East. As it leaves Tremonton and enters the town ofGarland 
 it becomes Main Street and continues 
north into the center of town. At the two-mile point, the route intersects 
Factory Street (the central east–west road in Garland) and turns east onto the 
street. From here the route continues east for 0.73 miles (1.17 km) out of 
Garland, then turns north onto 5400 West for less than half a mile before 
ending at its intersection withSR-13 .[
